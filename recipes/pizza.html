Cheese Pizza Recipe

If you love cheese pizza and cooking, you should make this recipe and fill your stomach!

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust
  • 0.5 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Place crust on baking sheet
  3. Spread pizza sauce on crust
  4. Sprinkle cheese evenly on top
  5. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until cheese is melted
  6. Eat and enjoy
