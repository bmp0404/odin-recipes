Cheese Quesadilla Recipe

If you love cheese quesadillas and cooking, you should make this recipe and fill your stomach!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • Salsa or sour cream, for dipping

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet
  3. Sprinkle cheese evenly on top
  4. Top with the second tortilla
  5. ook 2 to 3 minutes per side until golden and cheese melts
  6. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa or sour cream
